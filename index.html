ABOUT ME

Being born and raised in Los Angeles, my passion for web development and photography has been driven by the always-growing and up-to-date environment surrounding me.

Since graduating from the University of Michigan in April - where I obtained my Bachelor of Arts in Cognitive Science - I have been sharpening my fullstack development skills.

I love creating responsive web applications using Rails and PostgreSql on the backend with React.js, and Redux architecture on the frontend. I also write applications using the MERN stack (Mongo.DB, Express.js, Node.js and React.js).

Aside from development I'm an avid rock climber, traveler, and the best dad ever, to my cattle dog / Border Collie mix, Stella.
